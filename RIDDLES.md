# 20 Unsolvable Riddles

1. What has a mouth but never speaks, yet when it opens, everything inside disappears?

2. I am the beginning of eternity, the end of time and space, the beginning of every end, and the end of every race. What am I?

3. The man who makes it sells it. The man who buys it never uses it. The man who uses it never knows he's using it. What is it?

4. I am always coming but never arrive. What am I?

5. What can you hold in your left hand but not in your right hand?

6. The more you take, the more you leave behind. What am I?

7. I am light as a feather, yet the strongest person can't hold me for more than a minute. What am I?

8. What has keys but no locks, space but no room, and you can enter but can't go inside?

9. I am taken from a mine and shut in a wooden case, from which I am never released, yet I am used by almost everyone. What am I?

10. What grows when it eats, but dies when it drinks?

11. I have cities, but no houses live there. I have mountains, but no trees. I have water, but no fish. What am I?

12. What is always in front of you but can't be seen?

13. I am the ruler of shovels, I have a double. I am as thin as a knife, I have a wife. What am I?

14. What two things can you never eat for breakfast?

15. What has to be broken before you can use it?

16. I am not alive, but I grow. I don't have lungs, but I need air. I don't have a mouth, but water kills me. What am I?

17. What question can you never answer yes to?

18. What is so fragile that saying its name breaks it?

19. I am an odd number. Take away a letter and I become even. What number am I?

20. What has one eye, but cannot see?

---

*Note: These riddles are intentionally designed to have no clear solutions or are paradoxical in nature. They're meant to provoke thought rather than provide definite answers.*
